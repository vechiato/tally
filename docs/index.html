tally

A tool to help agents classify your bank transactions.

Works with GH Copilot, Claude Code, Codex, and other command-line based AI assistants
Tally works with your favorite AI coding assistant to automatically categorize transactions.

The Problem

WHOLEFDS MKT 10847 SEATTLE WA AMZN MKTP US*2K7X9 SQ *JOES COFFEE SEATTLE

Bank transactions look like gibberish. Your bank's categories are too broadâ€”"Shopping" when you need "Kids > Clothing" vs "Home > Furniture". Restaurants get lumped together when you want "Coffee" tracked separately from "Fast Food".

The Solution

AI assistants know what "WHOLEFDS MKT" is. They understand "SQ *" means Square. Just tell Tally your categorization rules in plain English:

"ZELLE to Sarah is babysitting â†’ Childcare" "COSTCO with GAS is fuel, otherwise groceries"

Tally works with your AI to write the rules to a simple file. No database, no cloud service. Just a file on your computer that you control.
